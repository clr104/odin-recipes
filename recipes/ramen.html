Ramen Recipe

Ramen is the Best.

Why? Because ramen is not only flavorful, but simple to make! Tastes the best during cold weather too! You can grab some noodles in the cabinet, your favorite protein. Stock, vegetables of your liking, and there ya go! This recipe only takes about 30 minutes. So let's start of with the ingridents.

Ingridents

  • Ramen Noodles. May come in the pack at your local market. Thinner noodles may have a better taste, but of course it is up to perference.
  • Stock
  • Seaweed (Optional)
  • Egg
  • Protein: Chicken, pork, or tofu
  • Vegetables: Seaweed,scallions, whatever you want!
  • Fishcake

Recipe Steps

  1. Bring stock to rolling boil.While the stock comes to a boil, separately boil an egg for 20 minutes.
  2. Add noodles and let cook for up to 3-4 mintues. Check and stir occasionally.
  3. Bring the heat to a simmer. Add vegetables & fishcake
  4. Cook your protein and check boiling egg. If egg done boiling, rinse the egg in cold water for about 10 minutes and peel.
  5. Slice egg in half.
  6. Add ramen to a boil and add sliced boil egg to the top of the ramen. Add seaweed in the bowl to the side or crumble it on top as a garnish!

See! Told you it was simple! Now snuggle up and enjoy your hot, tasty ramen. Thank you for joining us on another recipe.
