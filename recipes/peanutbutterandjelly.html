Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Who Doesn't Love Peanut Butter and Jelly?

If you're new to the peanut butter and jelly world, don't worry! We got you covered with a quick, 10 minute recipe for this classic sandwhich. It also only takes a few ingridents. Look below!

Ingridents

  • Bread of choice
  • Jelly or Jam of choice
  • Peanut Butter of choice (or Almond Butter if you want to make it healthy)

Recipe Steps

  1. Lay out two pieces of bread of choice
  2. Spread peanut butter (or almond butter) on one piece of bread.
  3. Spread jelly (or jam) on the other piece of bread.
  4. Put both pieces together. The jelly and peanut butter sides of the breads should be touching each other.

See! Told you it was simple! You can make this everyday, anytime.
