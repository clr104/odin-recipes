Fried Rice Recipe

Love Fried Rice? Here's how with this simple Fried Rice Recipe.

The recipe for fried rice is simple. You may even have these ingridients at home around your kitchen: chicken, beef, shrimp? Okay, shrimp is a bit too expensive to keep around these days, but you get the point. Also, you would need a good quality of rice, at least in my book. Look to your local Asian supermarket for these different types of rices. Some soy sauce, other sauces, a wok, and boom there we go. Remeber to cook the rice a day in advance. It tastes so much better when you do!

Okay, let's actually itemize the list of ingridients you need for the best fried rice.

  • Jasmine Rice, I personally use this kind.
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Protein of your choice
  • Peas and Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onions

Okay, so here is how you actually create the best fried rice:

  1. Step 1, cook the rice a day in advance. I don't know why fried rice tastes better after a day, but after testing both fresh cooked and cold rice, cold rice is better.
  2. Step 2, oil your wok (or pan) with seasme oil. When the wok gets to medium heat, scramble your eggs and put them to the side.
  3. Step 3, sautee the chopped garlic, peas and carrots, and white parts of the green onions. Sautee them for at least 1-2 mins.
  4. Step 4, add your rice and sautee for about 1-2mins. Add your soy sauce and oyster sauce and sautee for an additional 1 minute on high heat.
  5. When done, chop some of the green onion to garnish on top! And you have your fried rice!
